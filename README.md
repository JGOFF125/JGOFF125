## About

I am a **software development student at SAIT** and a product-focused engineer based in **Calgary, Alberta, Canada**. I build software with a strong emphasis on maintainability, user experience, security, and measurable business value.

My engineering interests span **full-stack development, mobile applications, applied artificial intelligence, cloud architecture, relational databases, developer tooling, and scalable product systems**. I enjoy working across the complete software lifecycle—from discovering the real problem and designing the architecture to implementation, testing, deployment, and continuous improvement.

I approach development with a product engineering mindset: understand the user, reduce unnecessary complexity, make deliberate technical decisions, and build solutions that can evolve beyond an initial prototype. My goal is not simply to make software work—it is to make it **reliable, understandable, secure, and useful**.

My AI and machine-learning work focuses on practical systems, including intelligent learning experiences, workflow automation, responsible AI integration, prompt engineering, retrieval-assisted applications, and cloud-based cognitive services.

- Full-stack, frontend, mobile, backend, and applied AI roles

- Product development partnerships with meaningful real-world impact
